# 📊 Online Retail Sales Analysis Dashboard (Microsoft Excel)

## Overview

This project is an end-to-end **data analytics portfolio project built entirely in Microsoft Excel** using an Online Retail dataset. The objective was to transform raw transactional data into a structured reporting solution that supports business decision-making through data cleaning, analysis, dashboarding, and insight generation.

The project covers the complete analytics workflow:

* Data cleaning and preparation
* Data transformation using Power Query
* Pivot table analysis
* Interactive dashboard creation
* Business insight generation
* Recommendation reporting

---

## Business Objective

The goal of this project is to analyze online retail sales performance and answer important business questions such as:

* How much revenue is being generated?
* Which products contribute most to sales?
* Which countries drive business growth?
* How does sales performance change over time?
* What trends can help improve future business decisions?

---

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains transactional online retail sales records including:

* Invoice information
* Product descriptions
* Quantity sold
* Unit prices
* Customer identifiers
* Country information

The analysis focuses on identifying sales patterns, customer behavior, geographic distribution, and product performance.

## Key Insights

### 1. Strong Revenue Performance

The business generated approximately **$18.6 million** in total revenue across nearly **20,000 invoices**, indicating a large transaction volume.

### 2. Seasonal Demand Pattern

Sales activity peaks during **September through November**, suggesting strong seasonal demand during the final quarter of the year.

### 3. Revenue Concentration in Top Products

Three products account for nearly **47% of total sales**, highlighting a significant concentration of revenue among a small number of items:

* DOTCOM POSTAGE
* REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
* PAPER CRAFT LITTLE BIRDIE

### 4. Geographic Dependence

Sales are heavily concentrated in domestic markets, accounting for approximately **90%** of total business activity.

### 5. International Expansion Opportunity

Although the business serves customers across **38 countries**, international markets contribute a smaller share relative to domestic sales, suggesting room for growth.

### 6. December 2011 Requires Careful Interpretation

The dataset may contain **incomplete records for December 2011**. Any decline observed during this period should therefore be interpreted cautiously and should not immediately be considered a true decrease in business performance.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Increase International Market Penetration

Since the company already serves customers in multiple countries, targeted international marketing campaigns could help diversify revenue sources.

### Reduce Dependence on Top Products

A significant share of revenue comes from only a few products. Expanding promotion of mid-performing products may reduce concentration risk.

### Prepare Inventory for Peak Season

Given the strong sales performance from **September to November**, inventory and logistics planning should prioritize this period.

### Improve Product Portfolio Analysis

Further segmentation of products by profitability and customer demand could support better inventory optimization.

### Monitor Seasonal Trends Continuously

Regular tracking of monthly trends can improve forecasting accuracy and business planning.
